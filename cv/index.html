Profile

Hi, I'm Jeffrey Moro

I work across academia, industry, and the arts to understand our media present and to build its future. I'm a skilled collaborator, with experience in project management, grant administration, events planning, and digital outreach. I'm trained in the arts, humanities, and sciences, and I can speak fluently the languages of the classroom, gallery, and command line. In my academic work and teaching, I investigate electronic literature, interactivity and games, software studies, and the history of media forms and technologies.
