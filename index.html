Hi, I'm Jeffrey Moro.
(That's my name over there too.)

I'm a researcher and artist currently employed by Five College Digital Humanities. I wear many different there: I run a fellowship and microgrant program, assist grant-funded projects, manage a speaker series, and create digital media resources.

I run projects and lead workshops about electronic literature, digital media history, and creative robotics. I'm occasionally asked to speak to crowds about introducing undergraduate students to digital project management and the digital humanities.
