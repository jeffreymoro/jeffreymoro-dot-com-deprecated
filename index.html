Hi, I'm Jeffrey Moro.
(That's my name over there too.)

I'm a researcher and artist currently employed by Five College Digital Humanities. I wear many different hats there: I run a fellowship and microgrant program, assist grant-funded projects, manage a speaker series, teach classes and workshops, and create digital resources. In my research, I work on electronic literature, interactivity and games, software studies, and the history of media forms and technologies. I'm here to help us understand our media present and build its future, informed by the intersecting histories of word, image, sound, and computation.

On this corner of the Internet, you can learn more about the projects I run, see a portfolio of my critical and creative work, check out my CV, or read some blog posts.
